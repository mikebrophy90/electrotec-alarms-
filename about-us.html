about us

Electrotec was first established in 1985 and based near Manchester. We specialise in the supply of burglar alarm systems and associated security products. Supplying everything from a door contact to a complete burglar alarm kit. As a purely online business we can keep our overheads a bit lower and pass on those savings to you our customers. Our motto is "keep it simple" which is why our web site is not cluttered with to much information crammed into small spaces. Each page has a similar layout making navigation through the site a breeze. Working with carefully selected manufactures we bring you quality products which are also great value. Where possible we source components which are manufactured in the UK, thereby giving support to the UK economy. We supply everything needed to install quality burglar alarms to professional standards, even the little things like screws, plugs, clips and staples. As a well established business we are committed to continuous improvement. As part of that commitment, our range of products will be tailored to meet demand. We always aim to get goods dispatched as quickly as possible, subject to receiving your cleared payment. We have a feedback page which allows you to express your thoughts and experiences using our site. A number of customers past and present have already added their comments for everyone to see. We are not some mysterious company that only exists in cyber space, we are real people. Contact details can be found on the "contact us" page.
